- # Positive vs. Negative Rights
	- Positive
		- Right to Life
		- Freedom of Speech
		- Right to a fair trial
		- Freedom of Assembly
		- Freedom of Expression
	- Negative
		- Right to Education
		- Right to shelter
		- Right to healthcare
